Strategic Governance Portal

Kerala’s skilling ecosystem is being reshaped through Naipunyolsavam, positioning it not just as a competition but as a structured movement to embed a strong skilling culture across the state. The initiative aims to elevate vocational skills to the same cultural importance as academics and arts by anchoring development at ministerial and secretariat levels.
List Visual

1. State Steering Council

Core Mandate

Acts as the highest strategic authority to provide the executive vision and inter-departmental synergy required for a statewide mission.

Primary Functions

  • Strategic Objective: Democratize the "Skilling" culture so it is recognized as a professional and cultural landmark.
  • Political Oversight: Chaired by the Chief Minister with Cabinet Ministers to ensure the mission remains a state-level priority.
  • Resource Alignment: Harmonizes different government arms to reach every household.

2. High Power Committee (HPC)

Core Mandate

The primary administrative body responsible for statewide policy implementation and inter-departmental executive decisions.

Primary Functions

  • Administrative Approval: Chaired by the Chief Secretary to vet and approve the Master Plan for statewide execution.
  • Mandatory Orders: Issues formal Government Orders (GOs) that bind departments to a single execution timeline.
  • Institutionalization: Ensures that the skilling mechanism is robust and independent of individual personnel changes.

3. Implementation Sub-Committee

Core Mandate

Serves as the bridge between administrative policy and technical field execution at the nodal agency level.

Primary Functions

  • Operational Oversight: Chaired by the Labour Secretary to monitor the progress and logistical readiness.
  • Frequent Monitoring: This committee meets more frequently than the HPC to monitor progress, resolve logistical hurdles, and ensure that the technical frameworks are being implemented according to the timeline.
  • Liaison: Coordinates directly with Directors and HODs for localized execution.

4. Program Committee

Core Mandate

The executive engine of Naipunyolsavam, responsible for field action and ground-level coordination.

Leadership & Function

  • Leadership: Led by the Mission Director and supported by the COO.
  • Execution: Responsible for the actual conduct, including venue mapping and candidate management.
  • Coordination: Manages various task units and ensures mandates are translated into ground-level action.

5. Specialized Task Units

Core Mandate

At the base of the hierarchy are the specialized units that handle the technical details of the competition. These units are categorized by their specific functions:

Primary Functions

  • Assessment: Managing 3 sets of question papers across 4 distinct levels for global benchmarks.
  • Industry Vetting: Direct integration of industrial assessors to ensure alignment with Occupational Standards.
  • Mobilization: Manages physical aspects of the competition across different districts and zones.
