Comprehensive Strategy

Naipunyolsavam 2026

Kerala’s skilling ecosystem is being reshaped through Naipunyolsavam, positioning it not just as a competition but as a structured movement to embed a strong skilling culture across the state. The initiative aims to elevate vocational skills to the same cultural importance as academics and arts, while integrating skill development into governance by anchoring it at ministerial and secretariat levels. This portal outlines the proposed governance structure, organisational framework, and key operational strategies to ensure its effective implementation and long-term sustainability.

1. State Steering Council

Core Mandate

Acts as the highest strategic authority to provide the executive vision and inter-departmental synergy required for a statewide mission.

Primary Functions

  • Strategic Objective: Democratize the "Skilling" culture so it is recognized as a professional and cultural landmark.
  • Political Oversight: Chaired by the Chief Minister with Cabinet Ministers (Labour, Industries, Higher Education, LSGD) to ensure the mission remains a state-level priority.
  • Resource Alignment: Harmonizes different government arms to reach every household.

2. High Power Committee (HPC)

Core Mandate

The primary administrative body responsible for statewide policy implementation and inter-departmental executive decisions.

Primary Functions

  • Administrative Approval: Chaired by the Chief Secretary to vet and approve the Master Plan for statewide execution.
  • Mandatory Orders: Issues formal Government Orders (GOs) that bind various departments (Education, Industries, Labour) to a single execution timeline.
  • Institutionalization: Ensures that the skilling mechanism is robust and independent of individual personnel changes.

3. Implementation Sub-Committee

Core Mandate

Serves as the bridge between administrative policy and technical field execution at the nodal agency level.

Primary Functions

  • Operational Oversight: Chaired by the Labour Secretary to monitor the "Skilling MIS" and logistical readiness.
  • Frequent Monitoring: Meets more regularly than the HPC to resolve immediate hurdles in candidate management and venue mapping.
  • Liaison: Coordinates directly with Directors and Heads of Departments (HODs) for localized execution.

4. Program Committee

Core Mandate

Serves as the executive engine of Naipunyolsavam, responsible for translating administrative mandates into field action.

Leadership & Function

  • Leadership: Led by the Mission Director and supported by executive officers such as the COO.
  • Execution: Responsible for the actual conduct of Naipunyolsavam, including venue mapping and candidate management.
  • Coordination: Manages various specialized task units and ensures secretary-level mandates are executed on the ground.

5. Specialized Task Units

Core Mandate

The technical engine responsible for executing the standardized assessment logic across 63 skills.

Primary Functions

  • Assessment Committee: Managing 3 sets of question papers across 4 distinct levels to maintain global benchmarks.
  • Industry Vetting: Direct integration of industrial assessors to ensure test projects align with market Occupational Standards.
  • Logistics and Mobilization Unit: Manages the physical aspects of the competition across different districts and zones.
