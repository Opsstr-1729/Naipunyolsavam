Strategic Skilling Kerala

Naipunyolsavam 2026

Naipunyolsavam 2026 is Kerala’s flagship skill movement designed to institutionalize a world-class skilling culture. By embedding proficiency at ministerial and secretariat levels, this mission democratizes technical excellence and celebratory professional success across the state.
List View Visual Map

1. State Steering Council

Core Mandate

Provides the highest strategic authority and inter-departmental synergy for the statewide mission.

Primary Functions

  • Chair: Chief Minister.
  • Vision: Democratize the "Skilling" culture as a cultural and professional landmark.
  • Alignment: Synchronizes different government arms to reach every household.

2. High Power Committee (HPC)

Core Mandate

The primary administrative body for statewide policy implementation and inter-departmental executive logic.

Primary Functions

  • Chair: Chief Secretary.
  • Administrative Approval: Vets and approves the Master Plan for statewide execution.
  • Mandatory Orders: Issues formal Government Orders (GOs) that bind departments to a single execution timeline.

3. Implementation Sub-Committee

Core Mandate

Bridges administrative policy and technical field execution at the SSDM level.

Primary Functions

  • Chair: Labour Secretary.
  • Oversight: Monitors the "Skilling MIS" logic and logistical readiness.
  • Monitoring: Resolves immediate hurdles in candidate management and venue mapping.

4. Program Committee

Core Mandate

The executive engine of Naipunyolsavam, responsible for field action and coordination.

Primary Functions

  • Leadership: Led by the Mission Director and supported by the COO.
  • Execution: Manages actual conduct, including venue mapping and ground coordination.
  • Liaison: Translates secretary-level mandates intoground-level deliverables.

5. Specialized Task Units

Core Mandate

The technical engine executing the standardized assessment logic for 63 skills.

Primary Functions

  • Assessment: Managing 3 sets of question papers across 4 levels for global benchmarking.
  • Industry Vetting: Direct integration of industrial assessors to align with Occupational Standards.
  • The Feeder System: Vetting talent to create an "industry-ready"verified pool.
